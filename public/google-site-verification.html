Google Search Console Verification

This page is for Google Search Console verification purposes.

Please add your Google Search Console verification meta tag to the main index.html file.

To verify your site with Google Search Console:

  1. Go to Google Search Console
  2. Add your property: https://www.handymangreeleyco.com
  3. Choose "HTML tag" verification method
  4. Copy the meta tag and add it to your index.html file in the <head> section
  5. Example: <meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR_VERIFICATION_CODE" />
